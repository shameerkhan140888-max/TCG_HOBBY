# Brand

## Brand Vision
TCG Hobby is a premium trading card platform for collectors, players, and retailers. The brand should feel trustworthy, fast, and commercially mature, with enough personality to feel distinctive without becoming playful or noisy.

The product experience should communicate:
- confidence for buying and selling
- clarity for browsing large catalogues
- enthusiasm for the hobby without hype
- operational credibility for staff and suppliers

## Brand Personality
- Premium
- Dark
- Precise
- Retail-first
- Collector-aware
- Operationally credible

## Typography
- Brand / headings: modern sans-serif, heavy weight, compact tracking
- Body: readable sans-serif with strong x-height
- Numeric data: tabular figures where supported

Typography rules:
- Headings should be bold and compact.
- Body copy should stay concise and legible.
- Avoid decorative fonts.
- Avoid overly wide letter spacing.
- Avoid viewport-based font scaling.

## Logo Usage Rules
- Use the approved horizontal TCG Hobby logo where space allows.
- The logo must sit directly on the dark header or dark surface.
- Never place the logo inside a card, pill, bordered square, or white panel.
- Never crop the logo or let surrounding containers clip its edges.
- Maintain generous transparent padding around raster assets.
- Use the stacked text wordmark only when the image logo is unavailable or unsuitable in constrained UI.
- Keep the logo clickable to the homepage.

## Brand Tone
- Direct, confident, and practical.
- Avoid marketing fluff.
- Avoid inflated claims about value, rarity, or investment outcomes.
- Use clear retail language and plain labels.
